About

Our Founder: Qari Abdinasir Farah


Qari Abdinasir Farah started his studies in the quranic sciences in Somalia and successfully memorized the Quran at a young age under his teachers Ustadh Abdirizak and Ustadh Cilmi. He then began to teach the Quran in Nairobi, Kenya before moving to the US where he would continue his passion of teaching the words of Allah. Qari Abdinasir has not only been teaching the Quran, but he has also continued his own education in the field of quranic sciences by reciting upon world renowned scholars such as Sheikh Waleed Al-Maneese, Sheikh Mahmoud Akawi, and Shiekh Ehaab Fikri. Qari Abdinasir has taught the Quran for well over a decade and has succeeded in producing over 20 huffadh. His students have gone on to secure multiple top positions in Quran competitions across the world such as Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Russia, Kuwait, and Dubai. Qari Abdinasir’s students are now studying the 10 Qiraat among other subjects while also training under him to become qualified Quran teachers themselves. Qari Abdinasir’s blessed journey with the Quran now continues with the opening of his own quranic center: Tibyan Center for Quranic Sciences.


Brief History of the Tibyan and it's origins


Tibyan Center first began as an aim to not only foster the first cohort of huffadh, but to create well rounded individuals with the passion and drive to continue bringing about cadres of huffadh, qurra, and imams. Fifteen years ago, the thought of having masses of people who can merely recite the Quran was an imagination, and the Muslim Community in Minnesota was in desperate need of Quranic centers with qualified teachers. It was then that Qari Abdinasir conceived the idea to bring about an unknown phenomenon to the West: students that memorize the Quran while holding on to their Islamic values. Not only was this dream achieved, but by the will of Allah (swt), dozens of his students have not only managed to memorize the Quran, but have gone on to compete in a plethora of Quran competitions across the world and have secured top positions. In addition to that, they have gone beyond memorizing the Quran, and are now studying for Ijaza certification in the 10 Qira’at, amongst other Islamic studies such as: Tafsir, Hadith, and Fiqh.Moving forward, Qari Abdinasir and his students are now expanding their reach to create an opportunity for not only dozens, but thousands of hopeful students that will continue to serve the Ummah for generations to come. The goal of Tibyan is to provide a nurturing environment for students who would like to pursue the field of Quranic sciences while instilling core Islamic teachings.
